Mamunpur

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Mamunpur village is 039053. Mamunpur village is located in Kharar Tehsil of Sahibzada Ajit Singh Nagar district in Punjab, India. It is situated 6km away from sub-district headquarter Kharar and 15km away from district headquarter Sahibzada Ajit Singh Nagar. As per 2009 stats, Mamu Pur is the gram panchayat of Mamunpur village.
The total geographical area of village is 192 hectares. Mamunpur has a total population of 682 peoples. There are about 126 houses in Mamunpur village. Kharar is nearest town to Mamunpur.
